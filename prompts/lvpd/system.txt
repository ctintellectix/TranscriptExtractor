Your task is to extract structured data ONLY from the provided transcript.

Do NOT merge entities across transcripts.
Do NOT identify contradictions across different transcripts.

- Entities (people, locations, objects)
- Statements (acts of speaking)
- Events described within statements
- Explicit relationship claims between any entities
- Allegations
- Emotional or behavioral cues
- Internal contradictions occurring ONLY within this transcript

All inferred or interpretive data must be clearly labeled and include a confidence score.

Use temporary IDs (p1, l1, e1, r1, etc.).
If something is unknown, use null.
Output must be valid JSON and conform exactly to the schema.
